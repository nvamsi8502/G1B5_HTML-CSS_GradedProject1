Baby Care

Feeding

Feed your baby with appropriate milk or formula. Follow a feeding schedule.

Diapering

Change diapers regularly to keep the baby dry. Use baby wipes and rash creams.

Sleeping

Create a safe and comfortable sleeping environment. Follow a sleep routine.

Bathing

Bathe your baby with mild baby soap and shampoo. Use a soft towel for drying.

Playtime

Engage in interactive play. Use safe and age-appropriate toys.

Health Check

Schedule regular check-ups with a pediatrician. Monitor baby's growth and development.

Safety Precautions

Ensure a baby-proof environment. Keep small objects away from the baby's reach.

Love and Affection

Provide plenty of love, cuddles, and affection. Create a strong emotional bond.
